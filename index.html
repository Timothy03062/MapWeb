Background

Mesh Block

Mesh block are statistical geographical data which are collected and processed and used for census purposes. Statistical Area 1 data used in Australian states and territories are spatial data constructed using these mesh blocks. The datasets which have been used here are "Number of Motor Vehicels per Dwellings" and "Selected Medians and Averages". Both censuses were taken in NSW, 2011 and 2016 respectively.


Data Journalism

The term "Data Journalism", refers to a form of journalism which retains core concepts of gathering, processing and distributing of information, but is instead done with digital tools. It encompasses an increased interaction between content creators or areas of collection with digital design, statistical analysis and so on. From this, users of such as data/information can make visualisations, and more accurate, informed predictions/reports.
